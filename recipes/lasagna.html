Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a fantastic mid-week meal. It's easy to prep ahead and is filling lunch or dinner. It even reheats well! Continue reading to learn how to make this wonderful for you and your loved ones. I'm sure it will become a family favorite in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Italian Ground Sausage
  • 1lb Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic (or more!)
  • 1 Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Tube of Cento Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons of Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Lasagna Noodle (store bought or homemade)
  • Parmesan
  • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps Overview

  1. Make Meat Sauce
  2. Cook Lasagna Noodles
  3. Make Ricotta Mixture
  4. Layer Lasagna
  5. Cover and Bake
  6. Rest
  7. Serve
