Fake Tacos Recipe

So my good friend Luis and his family are from Mexico. So growing up he had traditional Mexican tacos. When he and his family moved to the US he discovered the Americanized version of tacos. He is not a fan of places like Taco Bell so he's taken to calling these American Taco's "Fake Taco".

However, I am a good ole regular American so I like our "Fake Tacos". Do I prefer a traditional taco most of the time? Yes. But I also like the ease and convenience of American tacos. Which is why I have this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Hard Taco Shells
  • 1 pound of Ground Beef
  • Your favorite package of Taco Seasoning
  • Shredded Mexican Cheese Blend
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato (optional
  • Sour Cream

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook Taco Shells according to package instructions
  2. Dice the yellow onion
  3. Saute half the onions in a oiled pan on medium heat
  4. Once the onions are translucent add in the ground beef
  5. Once the ground beef is cooked through take the pan off the heat and add in your favorite taco seasonings.

Assemble Your Taco

Now for the fun. Simply fill taco shell with your favorite topping. I tend to do a lot of cheese and sour cream. Yum!
